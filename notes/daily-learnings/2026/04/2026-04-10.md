### Three-Dimensional Evaluation Framework

**Dimension A — New Trending (Growth Indicator)**
- 24h stars > 200 (anomalously high relative to language average)
- First appeared on trending today
- Growth rate in top 20%

**Dimension B — Substantial Value (Fundamentals)**
- README > 500 characters
- Not a toy project (has clear functionality)
- Commit within last 30 days

**Dimension C — Medium-Large Scale (Durability)**
- commits > 50
- Single language > 1000 lines
- Multiple contributors
- Has formal CI/CD or docs/

**Scoring Logic**:
| Rating | Condition | Stars |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Top | A+B+C all met | ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️ |
| High | A+B met | ⭐️⭐️⭐️⭐️ |
| Normal | B+C met | ⭐️⭐️⭐️ |
| Watch | Only A or only C | ⭐️⭐️ |
| Skip | Others | — |
