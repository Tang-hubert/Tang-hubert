### NemoClaw vs OpenClaw Comparison

| Aspect | OpenClaw | NemoClaw |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Target** | Independent devs, makers | Enterprise |
| **Stack** | TypeScript/Node.js | Python/NeMo |
| **Skills** | 5,000+ community skills | Enterprise tool integration |
| **Security** | Historically weak (900+ malicious skills, API leaks) | Secure-by-design, confidential computing |
| **Hardware** | 1.5GB RAM Mac Mini | GPU-accelerated servers |
| **Status** | Production-ready | Coming at GTC 2026 |

**Recommendation**:
- Independent devs → **OpenClaw** (fast to deploy, add your own security)
- Enterprises → **Wait for NemoClaw** (formal release pending)
