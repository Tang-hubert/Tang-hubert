**Background:** Hubert wants to post daily music recommendation reels on IG, with 9:16 format (1080×1920), sourced from YouTube Music Videos.

**Research Questions:**
1. **How to automatically identify trending segments?** — IG Reels trending audio has "user-wanted segments" (hook/chorus/emotional peaks, energy peaks, beat phrase starts)

2. **How to make 9:16 cropping more harmonious?** — Direct crop cuts off singer/musician, making画面突兀

### Hot Segment Detection
- Researched Instagram trending audio mechanism (split tool, trending sounds library)
- Searched YouTube clip highlight extraction tools (n8n/Gemini AI + FFmpeg)
- Researched librosa audio processing (Energy Peak Detection, Beat Tracking, Onset Detection)
- Final algorithm: **RMS Energy Peak Detection**:
  - Calculate per-frame RMS energy → rolling mean smoothing → find local maxima
  - Scoring: `energy × duration_bonus (15-20s=1.2×) × beat_alignment (within 200ms=1.3×)`
  - Deduplicate and take top-N

### Phrase Boundary Fix
- Problem: Energy peak algorithm抓到的高能量段, head/tail may be in middle of lyrics sentence, causing "sudden silence"
- Solution: `librosa.onset.onset_detect(backtrack=True)` finds phrase start points
- Auto-alignment: Segment head aligned to onset time minus PREROLL(0.5s), segment tail aligned to last onset plus PAD_MIN(0.3s)
- Test Seg01: 103.05s → 103.29s (aligned 0.24s forward to phrase)

### 9:16 Visual Processing
- Problem: Direct crop cuts off main subject (singer centered left/right)
- Researched Ken Burns Effect (slow zoom-out + pan): zoom 1.06→1.14
- Tested FFmpeg zoompan filter (FFmpeg 6.1 verified success)
